Lineage:
Process step:
Process description:
Spatial files were originally obtained from the US Census Bureau's 2000 TIGER/Line tracts for the five boroughs for New York City.  Using definitions from the NYCHVS (http://www.census.gov/hhes/www/housing/nychvs/nychvs.html), tracts were selected, tract boundaries dissolved, and information added manually.
